💡 Tips for Your Situation

Start with Airbus — it's built for your industry

The Airbus Supply Chain Specialist cert uses aerospace as its primary case study throughout. Even with your CPIM, the aerospace-specific framing of MPS, MRP, S&OP, and procurement makes it uniquely relevant. It also gives you a credential directly from the industry's biggest name.

Coursera Plus (~$59/mo) is worth it here

With 7 specializations and multiple standalone courses, Coursera Plus pays for itself quickly. Self-paced access across everything is ideal when you're balancing a demanding job and a newborn at home.

Green Belt is saved for last — intentionally

Green Belt is placed at the end so you bring your full toolkit to it — SCM, AI, Yellow Belt, and leadership experience all make you a stronger Green Belt candidate. Finishing with the ASQ CSSGB gives you the most recognized quality credential in manufacturing, and combined with CPIM it's an exceptionally rare combination in aerospace.

AI is already reshaping your job — get ahead of it

AI-driven demand forecasting, predictive maintenance, and production scheduling are moving into aerospace manufacturing now. No coding experience is required for the AI courses in this roadmap — they're built for ops professionals, not developers.
